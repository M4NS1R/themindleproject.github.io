FAQS

What are you all about?


This is a project which tries to bring young developers together. It creates a platform for young minds to mingle. You can upload your project and other developers can view it or even offer to collaborate on it. You can also view other projects.

Do you have an age limit?

Even though we created this platform with young developers in mind, ages 16-28, we do not have age restrictions as to who can join. Anyone of any gender, age or educational background can register with us.

What do you mean by upload project?

When we talk about uploading a project we don't mean uploading the actual sourcecode. What it means is that you can upload details of your project: What it's about, it's github repository, a few screenshots and maybe even a link to a demo. People who wish to collaborate with you can then send you an email.

Why do I have to create an account?

It is necessary for you to create an account before viewing other people's projects. This is for privacy matters and also to enable us grow our userbase. We do not collect any user information. You can find a link to our privacy policy in the footer

Is it free?

Registration is free and will always be free. We have not included any ads whatsoever, however, we might opt to include ads to our mobile platform when we decide to launch it.

What is this Mindle Project?

This is a project which tries to bring young developers together. It creates a platform for young minds to mingle. Even though we built this platform mainly for young developers, between ages 16 - 28, we do not have age restriction. Anyone can join.
